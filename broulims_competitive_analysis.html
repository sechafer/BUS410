Broulim’s Competitive Advantage

Strategic Analysis & Recommendations

Using financial benchmarking and the 4+3 framework

Goal: Identify how Broulim’s can compete like Sprouts — with a premium edge.

Competitor Overview (2024)

Sources: Walmart, Sprouts, Kroger Annual Reports 2024

Who Has the Advantage?

  • Sprouts has the highest operating margin: 6.7%
  • Type: Premium Advantage — not lower cost, but higher price
  • Customers pay more for organic, fresh, and niche

Recommendations for Broulim’s

  3. ESOP Branding
    Promote 49% employee ownership

Conclusion

  • Sprouts wins on premium, not price
  • Broulim’s can replicate this via fresh + local + culture
  • Next step: pilot premium fresh store with local storytelling
